Social Awareness & Safety Tips

Protect yourself and others from air pollution with these essential tips

Wear Protective Masks

When pollution levels are high, wearing a proper mask can significantly reduce your exposure to harmful particles:

  • Use N95 or N99 masks for maximum protection
  • Ensure proper fit with no gaps around the edges
  • Replace masks regularly as per manufacturer instructions
  • Avoid using cloth masks as they offer limited protection against PM2.5

Improve Indoor Air Quality

Create a safe indoor environment with these measures:

  • Use air purifiers with HEPA filters
  • Keep windows closed during high pollution periods
  • Add indoor plants that naturally purify air
  • Regularly clean and vacuum to reduce dust accumulation
  • Avoid smoking or burning incense indoors

Reduce Personal Pollution Contribution

Everyone can help reduce air pollution through simple lifestyle changes:

  • Use public transportation or carpool when possible
  • Walk or cycle for short distances
  • Maintain your vehicle with regular servicing
  • Switch to electric vehicles if possible
  • Avoid unnecessary idling of vehicles
